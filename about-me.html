HOBBIES AND INTERESTS

BASKETBALL

Basketball is one of my favorite sports and pastimes. I usually go to an outdoor basketball court with my brother and we play or practice together. I really enjoy the fast paced thrill of a basketball game as well as the satisfaction of sinking a shot. Not only that, but it also helps calm me down when I am stressed by being able to release my energy on the court. Although I love basketball, I don't follow any professional leagues.


Basketball Basics/Objectives (go to https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rules_of_basketball to learn more):
  1. 2 teams have their own sides and their own nets (hoops/baskets). They must prevent opponents from scoring (getting the ball into their net).
  2. Dribble the ball by bouncing it on the ground with your hands. DON'T move with the ball without dribbling ("travel" is an illegal move).
  3. Shoot the ball into the opponent's net (jump and throw the ball)
  4. If the opponents have possesion, make sure they can't get past you and score (no pushing or shoving!).
